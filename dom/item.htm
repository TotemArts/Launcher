Welcome Back Commander!

First of all, we are happy to welcome back our long-time community developer @Handepsilon, who is now the newest member of the Totem Arts development team! He already has made some great improvements for this patch such as improved bot AI and an updated damage UI system! Also @Fffreak9999, @SMayhew, @SonnyX and @Xelus have joined the team and will be supporting us as much as they can!

(Known bugs: Eyes has lighting and paths need rebuild warning, respawn timer when dead doesn't update)

| Features |

  • New Scoring System:
    • Added a new point system by default that more closely follows the Offensive/Defensive and Support score system, so MVPs should be those with the highest total scores on the board as well
    • Points and credits are NOT tied to one another using the new system
    • Can be toggled back to legacy by servers
  • Medium Tank:
    • Added Coaxial MG by default

| Balance |

  • Cruise Missile: Decreased health from 100 to 90
  • 500 Sniper Rifle: Increased headshot multiplier from x3.25 to x3.5
  • Snipers: Decreased damage vs. aircraft from 0.3x to 0.2x
  • Guard Tower / AA-Tower: Increased health from 1000 to 1250
  • Turret / SAM-Site: Increased health from 1000 to 1250
  • Orca:
    • Increase Rockets clip size from 4 to 8
    • Reduced Rocket base damage from 53 to 35
    • Increased rate of fire of Rockets from 2/s to 2.5/s
    • Reduced MG damage multiplier for MCTs from 3.0x to 0.75x
    • Gun head shot damage reduced from 2.5x to 1.5x
    • Gun splash radius reduced from 130 to 85
    • Reduced Base speed from 775 to 700
    • Reduced Boost Speed from 968 to 800
  • Apache:
    • Reduced rate of fire of Rockets from 6.6/s to 5.0/s
    • Gun head shot damage reduced from 5.0x to 1.5x
    • Gun splash radius reduced from 150 to 85
    • Reduced rocket reload time from 8 seconds to 6 seconds
    • Reduced Base speed from 775 to 700
    • Reduced Boost Speed from 968 to 800
    • Reduced Gun damage against buildings to match Orca's DPS
    • Increased Gun damage against vehicles to match Orca's DPS

| Bug Fixes |

  • Fixed getting stuck on zoomed mode when entering vehicle / getting character crate
  • Fixed switching to first person camera when zooming / ironsighting when entering vehicle / getting character crate
  • Removed a dot in the spotting messages that came after "[EPIC]"
  • Fixed issues with kill assists not working on high-damage weapons
  • Fixed Offensive / Defensive assists being given to the person who died, not the one who shot them
  • Fixed a grave oversight with calculating vehicle damage for MVP stats
  • Fixed the vote kick menu
  • Air units unlock their yaw when their pilot leaves
  • Fixed another instance that could lead to becoming a 'one-armed bandit'.
  • Fixed overview map increasingly filling texture memory when opened
  • Fixed the Orca/Apache not alternating their missile bays properly
  • GDI Chinooks will deliver GDI airdrops
  • Fixed team-based airstrike backattachment materials

| Visuals & Audio |

  • Added a unique vignette for burning and tiberium bleed
  • Added camera transition when entering / exiting vehicles
  • Marksman Rifle: New Scope texture, disabled zooming
  • Added a new boink alternative sound effect called 'Modern'

| Quality of Life |

  • Damage vignette will no longer remain on screen unless the player's health is below 25
  • Improved bot AI! They attempt to disarm beacons, repair buildings, and are generally more aggressive on offense
  • You'll exit your vehicle on the opposite side of where you're turret is point towards from now on
  • After you get killed, the camera rotates and zooms to the killers location
  • Added an AFK indicator. In 3 minutes, the player will get a prefix in front of their nickname
  • Bots display their skill number in the name
  • Spies and stolen vehicles will be marked with their own team color. In addition, stolen vehicles will have the tag 'STOLEN'.
  • Added command to toggle targeting box / HP bar [ToggleTargettingBox]
  • Stopped harvesters should go back to being stopped after they're destroyed
  • Harvesters can be given 'stop' orders while harvesting/dumping tiberium. They still have to finish, but will then go to the standby waypoint
  • Changed Default Map Exclusion from 3 to 9 to allow more maps to cycle through before they are allowed back in for voting
  • You now keep credits when changing team.
